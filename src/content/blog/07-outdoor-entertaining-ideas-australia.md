## Tip 1: Size the Dining Table for Your Maximum, Not Your Average

The outdoor dining table is the centrepiece of the entertaining area, and undersizing it is the most common and most consequential mistake I see. I bought a 6-seater for a space that consistently needs 10, and spent two years hosting gatherings where people were squeezed or eating in shifts.

**The formula:** 60cm per person of table length. For 8: 240cm. For 10: 300cm. For 12: 360cm or two tables joined.

My current setup: a 240cm powder-coated aluminium table from Temple & Webster ($689) that comfortably seats 8 and pushes to 10 with a fold-out extension table from Kmart ($149) joined at the end.
